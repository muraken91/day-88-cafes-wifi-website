About Me

Just someone that enjoys working in a cafe.

Started building this page as part of 100 days of code: python bootcamp project, but working in a cafe has always been enjoyable for me, there are so many projects that I managed to finish while working inside a cafe. Thus, I thought, why not make it a personal blog and a place for people to share their experience of working in cafe.

Everyone can add their favorite cafe into this list, while I'm starting with just Jakarta, once there are bigger community, I would love to expand it to other cities too. Definitely, inspired by laptopfriendly.co (which you guys should check out if you're living overseas), which was the blueprint for this project, but since I really enjoy building this, I would like to be able to create a similar platform for Jakarta, with a bit of a more personal touch in a bloglike environment.

I hope that I can build a community for people that wants to share their experience of working in a cafe. From popular places (looking at you starbucks), to more of a small coffeeshop that allows you to interact with the barista, and many more. Hope you enjoy this site!
